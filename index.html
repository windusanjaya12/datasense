Selected Client Engagements

Real-world analytics projects that helped business owners improve visibility, strengthen decision-making, and uncover growth opportunities.

Construction · B2B & Retail

Building Materials Distributor

Delivered · Full engagement
Business Challenge

Management had thousands of SKUs but no visibility into which products were profitable, which inventory was stagnant, or where margins were being eroded. Purchasing decisions were driven by intuition rather than data.

Key Insights Uncovered
  • 18% of inventory had not moved in over 90 days, silently tying up working capital
  • A small group of products was generating the majority of total revenue
  • Several high-volume SKUs carried below-average margins, eroding overall profitability
Decisions Made by Management
  • Reduced purchasing of slow-moving items to free up cash
  • Reallocated inventory budget toward fast-moving, high-margin products
  • Adjusted pricing on underperforming SKUs to protect margin
  • Focused sales effort on the highest-performing product categories
Business Impact
  • Reduced cash tied up in slow-moving inventory
  • Improved inventory turnover across key categories
  • Strengthened purchasing decisions with clear data
  • Increased visibility into product-level profitability
Retail · Convenience Store

Multi-Location Convenience Store Chain

Delivered · Full engagement
Business Challenge

Operating multiple stores without a consolidated view made it impossible to compare performance across locations, detect inventory discrepancies, or make timely decisions. Reviews were manual, slow, and unreliable.

Key Insights Uncovered
  • Certain stores were consistently underperforming relative to the rest of the network
  • Recurring stock discrepancies were detected across multiple locations
  • Product mix performance varied significantly by store, revealing optimisation opportunities
Decisions Made by Management
  • Launched investigation into inventory discrepancies at flagged locations
  • Optimised product assortment by store based on local performance data
  • Increased management focus on consistently underperforming locations
  • Standardised reporting across all stores for faster weekly reviews
Business Impact
  • Faster management reviews across all locations
  • Improved operational accountability at store level
  • Better inventory control and reduced discrepancy exposure
  • More confident, data-driven decisions at every level
